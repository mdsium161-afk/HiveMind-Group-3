# Research Plan — Group 3 (Christoph Dashboard)

## Focus Area
Area A — "Christoph" Dashboard: reducing cognitive load, improving navigation, and creating a clear unified dashboard for workplace professionals.

## Research Objectives
1. Validate Christoph's pain points with real workplace professionals (not students)
2. Understand how people currently find and organise information at work
3. Identify what a unified dashboard must surface to reduce daily friction
4. Test whether HiveMind's proposed AI assistant would be trusted by professionals

---

## Target Participants

| Criteria | Detail |
|---|---|
| Occupation | Working professionals with ≥6 months workplace experience |
| Seniority | Junior to Senior (varied to capture range) |
| Sectors | Tech, Banking, Telecoms, Real Estate, Civil Engineering |
| Company size | Small (11–50), Medium (50–100), Large (1000+) |
| Exclusion | Students currently enrolled in full-time education |

**Target number:** 6–8 participants across the group (each interviewer speaks to at least 2).

## Interview Questions

All groups use the same guiding question set so findings can be compared cohort-wide.

### Section A — Role & Company Context
1. What is your current job role or title?
2. What industry is your company in?
3. Roughly how big is the company?

### Section B — Onboarding & First Weeks
4. Think about the last time you started a job or changed teams. What did the first few days/weeks look like?
5. In the first two weeks, what were the top 3 things you needed to know to do your job well?
6. What was the hardest part of getting started?
7. Did onboarding feel helpful or more like box-ticking?

### Section C — Finding Information at Work
8. When you need an answer at work, where do you go first? (people, email, Teams/Slack, SharePoint, Google Drive, etc.)
9. How often do you have to check more than one place?
10. What is annoying about that?
11. Tell me about a time you could not find something important.
12. How do you personally keep track of important links or documents?
13. If you had one home screen at work, what would you want it to show?
14. What information should be super quick to reach?
15. When you find a document, how do you know it is correct?

### Section D — Starting a Job
16. On your first day, what would have made you feel less lost?
17. In your first week, did you clearly know what to do next?
18. What makes onboarding feel personal and useful?
19. What made you feel like you were on track?
20. When you needed help, did you know who to ask?

### Section E — AI Helper Trust
21. Have you used AI tools at work? What was good or bad about them?
22. What would make you trust an AI answer?
23. What would make you not trust it?
24. What questions should AI redirect to a human?
25. Would you worry about privacy if AI used your company data?

### Section F — Wrap-up
26. What were the top 2 problems you mentioned today?
27. What do those problems cost you (time, stress, mistakes)?
28. If you could change one thing to make starting a job easier, what would it be?
