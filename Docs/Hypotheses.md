# Hypotheses — Group 3 (Christoph Dashboard)

Based on synthesis of 6 user interviews and the Christoph persona pain points (cognitive load, scattered information, difficult navigation, no unified view), we formed the following testable hypotheses.

---

## Hypothesis 1 — Unified Dashboard Reduces Cognitive Load

> **We believe that** a single unified dashboard showing tasks, key documents, team contacts, and AI suggestions **will help Christoph** reduce the time spent context-switching between tools **because** 100% of our interview participants reported checking 2+ separate platforms daily to find basic information, and described this as "breaking focus" and "frustrating."

**Metric:** Task completion under 3 clicks; time-on-task ≤ 90 seconds for finding critical information.

---

## Hypothesis 2 — Role-Tailored Onboarding Increases Confidence

> **We believe that** a structured, role-specific onboarding flow with daily milestones and clear "what's next" prompts **will help Christoph** feel prepared and confident within his first week **because** 5 of 6 participants described generic onboarding as "box-ticking" that did not relate to their actual responsibilities, and 4 reported not knowing their priorities for the first 1–2 weeks.

**Metric:** Self-reported confidence score ≥ 4/5 after experiencing the prototype onboarding flow; users can state their "next task" unprompted.

---

## Hypothesis 3 — Source Transparency Builds AI Trust

> **We believe that** displaying the source document, last-updated date, and a confidence indicator alongside every AI answer **will help Christoph** trust the AI assistant's recommendations **because** every participant who had used AI at work cited "I don't know where it gets the answer from" as their primary reason for distrust, and 3 participants explicitly asked for source links before acting on AI output.

**Metric:** Trust Perception score ≥ 4/5 in post-test questionnaire; users can correctly identify the origin of an AI answer within 30 seconds.

---

## Hypothesis 4 — Centralised Knowledge Hub Cuts Information Search Time

> **We believe that** a searchable, role-filtered Knowledge Hub with version-controlled documents **will help Christoph** find verified information in under 2 minutes **because** participants reported spending 5–30 minutes per search session across multiple platforms, with a high risk of acting on outdated document versions.

**Metric:** Time-on-task for "find the compliance policy" scenario ≤ 2 minutes; 0 instances of selecting a deprecated document during testing.

---

## Hypothesis 5 — Visual Navigation Structure Reduces Overwhelm

> **We believe that** a left-rail navigation with clearly labelled, colour-coded sections (Dashboard, Knowledge Hub, AI Assistant, My Progress) **will help Christoph** navigate the platform without instruction **because** participants described cognitive overload when presented with tool interfaces that lack clear hierarchy, and expressed a preference for "everything I need to start my day in one view."

**Metric:** System Usability Scale (SUS) score ≥ 80; first-click accuracy ≥ 80% on navigation tasks.
