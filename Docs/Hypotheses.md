Hypothesis 
 

New employees in smaller companies tended to struggle with onboarding due to fragmented information and a lack of structured guidance, leading them to rely heavily on colleagues and informal communication like teams or communication with colleagues 


I found that using AI as a central hub in finding information could be very useful when looking for certain information within the onboarding. It could 

Reduce time to competency (potentially within first 2 weeks)  

Decrease reliance on colleagues for basic questions  

Improve confidence and task completion speed for new hires
